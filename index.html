Our Story

St. Marys Institute for Future Skills was founded by the visionary leader Dr. Rev. K. V. K. Rao. In the year 1996, his vision gave rise to Joseph Sri Harsha & Mary Indraja Educational Society, which currently has a strong foothold of 28 institutions offering programs to students by providing quality education to urban, semi-urban & rural students of India and Abroad. SMIFS has spread across 8 states in the country under his able leadership, providing rural youth skill development programs sponsored by the Ministry of Rural Development, GoI.
